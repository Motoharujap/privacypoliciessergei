Privacy Policy

Company: Exponential Dog Effective: April 8, 2026

1 Introduction

Exponential Dog ("we", "our", or "us") built EasyFridge: Fast Product Scan as a mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app and the choices you have associated with that data.

By using EasyFridge: Fast Product Scan you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2 Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Photos — EasyFridge: Fast Product Scan takes photos of food items and their expiry labels using your device camera. These photos are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Product notes — Optional names or notes you enter for fridge items are stored locally on your device.

Data Collected Automatically

Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymised usage data, including app opens, feature interactions, and crash events. This data helps us understand how users interact with the app and improve the user experience. Firebase Analytics may collect device identifiers, approximate location (country/region), and usage patterns. Data is processed by Google LLC under their privacy policy.

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports when the app encounters an error. Crash reports may include device model, OS version, app version, and a stack trace. No personally identifiable information is intentionally included in crash reports.

Google AdMob

EasyFridge: Fast Product Scan displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers and usage data to serve personalised or contextual ads. You can opt out of personalised ads through your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads on Android). AdMob data is processed by Google LLC under their privacy policy.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscription status. RevenueCat may collect your device identifier and purchase history to verify entitlements. RevenueCat does not receive your payment card details — those are handled entirely by the Google Play Store.

3 Camera Permission

📷 android.permission.CAMERA

EasyFridge: Fast Product Scan requires access to your device camera to photograph food items and scan barcodes. Camera access is used solely within the app for this purpose. We do not access your camera in the background or transmit images off your device.

4 Microphone Permission

🎙️ android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO

EasyFridge: Fast Product Scan requests microphone access to allow you to speak expiry dates as an alternative to manual entry. Voice input is processed entirely on-device using the Android Speech Recognition API. We do not record, store, or transmit your voice.

5 Internet Access

EasyFridge: Fast Product Scan requires internet access to:

  • Look up product names from the Open Food Facts database when scanning barcodes (a public, open-source food database)
  • Display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Send anonymised analytics and crash reports via Firebase

No personal data is transmitted to Exponential Dog's own servers.

6 Data Storage

All fridge data — photos, expiry dates, and notes — is stored locally on your device in a private app database. This data is not backed up to the cloud by us and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

EasyFridge: Fast Product Scan is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.
