Privacy Policy – Echoes of Europa

Effective Date: November 17, 2025

Echoes of Europa (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Android app, Echoes of Europa.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal data such as your name, email address, or date of birth. You can use the app without creating an account.

2. Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about how the app is used, including:

  • Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Anonymous analytics for app performance and usage

This information is aggregated and anonymized, and is only used to improve the app.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect usage information:

  • Firebase Crashlytics – for crash reporting and app performance monitoring
  • AdMob by Google – for displaying ads

These services are governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • AdMob/Google Privacy Policy

We do not share your personal information with these services; only anonymized app usage data is sent.

4. Advertising

Our app may display ads through AdMob. AdMob may collect non-personal data to serve relevant ads. We do not control the collection of data by third-party ad networks. Please review their policies directly.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect app usage data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Echoes of Europa is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the app and the effective date will be revised.
