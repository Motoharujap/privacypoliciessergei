Privacy Policy

App: PuppyPaw: Dog Clock Widget
Developer: Exponential Dog
Effective date: June 7, 2026

Thank you for using PuppyPaw: Dog Clock Widget. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user. We take your privacy seriously and have designed PuppyPaw to work entirely on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. PuppyPaw operates entirely on your device. No account is required, no analytics SDK is included, and no data is sent to any server operated by Exponential Dog or any third party.

2. Device Permissions

PuppyPaw may request the following permission:

  • Read media images (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) — only if you choose to display photos from your own device gallery in the widget. This permission is optional. If you do not use the "use my own photos" feature, this permission is never requested. Accessed photos are displayed locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

No other device permissions are required or requested.

3. Local Storage

PuppyPaw saves your preferences (such as widget style and chosen photos) locally on your device using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed when you uninstall the app.

4. Advertising

PuppyPaw contains no advertisements and does not integrate any advertising SDK.

5. In-App Purchases

PuppyPaw does not offer any in-app purchases, subscriptions, or paid features at this time.

6. Third-Party Services

PuppyPaw does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

7. Children's Privacy

PuppyPaw does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted at the same URL and the effective date above will be updated. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
