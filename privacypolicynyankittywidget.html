Last updated: February 5, 2026

Welcome to Catsies Clock Widget! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you use Catsies Clock Widget, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as features used and time spent in the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics purposes.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Catsies Clock Widget does not collect:

The information we collect is used to:

Third-Party Services

Catsies Clock Widget uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. Firebase may collect device information and usage data. For more information, please visit Firebase Analytics Privacy Policy.

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to show personalized ads. For more information, please visit Google Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Your app preferences (such as selected frame style and refresh interval) are stored locally on your device. We do not store personal data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Catsies Clock Widget is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app displays cat images and does not contain any inappropriate content.

Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
